LiteGApps is a reduced size Google application package. LiteGApps was developed for use on devices with limited internal memory or devices running a LiteGApps includes only the most essential Google apps like Google Play Store, Google Maps and Gmail, so it's smaller in size compared to the standard Google apps suite. However, some features may not be available on LiteGApps. LiteGApps is developed by independent developers and hosted on websites such as XDA-Developers.com.
