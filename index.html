LiteGapps is an Custom Gapps for Android Operation System, free and open-source software. by providing complete, stable support and several additional features.

small friendly complete

LiteGapps first release on 12 july 2020 supports android Lollipop Up to Android 14. comes with 5 products namely : LiteGapps, LiteGapps++, LiteGapps Controller, LiteGapps Remover LiteGapps Addon

LiteGapps was originally a small and friendly custom gapps, over time we have added a lot of device support and several packages Variant,Addon and LiteGapps Remover to complete user needs.

Now LiteGApps is a unique distribution that covers a wide variety of use cases. You can install it through recovery, or as a Magisk module.

Features

• Support system and systemless

• Can be installed in recovery, magisk manager, FKM flasher

• Small size 43MB (lite)

• Support adb sideload

• 75+ addon/package support

• Support architecture arm,arm64,x86,x86_64

• Support android 5.0 to 13.0

• Open source (MIT)

• Supports installation/uninstall in one flashable ZIP

• Long Term Support (LTS)

• Support Raspberry

• Support GSI/SGSI

• Supports various arch arm, arm64 and android 5.0 to 13.0 In one flashable ZIP package (LiteGapps++)

• Support install/uninstall without wipe/data

• OTA Update Support

• There are 8 variants lite,core,user,go,basic,nano,micro,pixel

• Rich features and tweaks that can be enabled in the litegapps controller

LiteGapps++

LiteGapps++ is a development of regular LiteGapps, with LiteGapps++ you can use it for cross-architectures and android versions in 1 flashable ZIP, while maintaining its small size. It has 3 variants, namely Reguler,LTS,MicroG.
