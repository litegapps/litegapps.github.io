Introduction

LiteGapps litegapps is specifically targeted for a specific Android/Architecture, with 8 variants : Lite,Core,Go,User,Basic,Nano,Micro,Pixel.

Description

LiteGapps is our first product that has complete support and is updated the most actively from our other products.

at first litegapps only had one variant, namely the lite variant, over time we have added variants that can be integrated by our addon.

litegapps also supports a fairly complete architecture, namely arm32, arm64, x86_64, x86
